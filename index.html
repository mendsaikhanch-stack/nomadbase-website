About Nomad Base

Where Ancient Nomad Culture Meets Modern Remote Work

Nestled in the heart of Mongolia, Nomad Base is a retreat designed for digital professionals seeking inspiration, focus, and a connection to one of the world's last great wilderness frontiers.

🏔️

5.5 Hectare Campus

Sprawling grounds with walking trails, meditation spots, and open steppe views.

📡

Starlink Internet

Reliable 100+ Mbps connectivity via Starlink satellite — video calls, uploads, no problem.

🍲

Community Dining

Fresh Mongolian cuisine daily. Communal meals bring nomads together every evening.

🐎

Cultural Experiences

Horse riding, eagle hunting demos, nomad family visits — the real Mongolia.
